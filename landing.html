Graphic Design

A collection of my projects in Graphic Design 1-2.

Basic Shapes Animal

This is my first project in Graphic Design that I created using basic shapes. The main character is Charlie the Bunny who is stranded in the middle of a iceberg. He is surrounded by his friends, Norry the narwhal and Pingu the penguin.

Self Portrait

This image is pretty self-explanatory. Illustrator allowed me to create shapes and shades to create a realistic illustration of my face, mainly using the pen tool. I attempted to create some highlights and shading underneath my eyes and near my cheeks.

Personal Logo: Graphic

I wanted my company logo to be kind of representative of who I am as a person. This image demonstrates the process through which I was able to combine certain aspects of my life such as music, art, and travelling into a graphic. The colors also demonstrate how colorful I am as a person.

Personal Logo: Typographic

My company is called Jewel Productions. This typographic logo only demonstrates the font type of my design.I named this Jewel Productions so, I wanted my typographic logo to be somewhat soft and curvy to make it more appealing.

Personal Logo: Logo Type

This was one of my favorite projects in the entire class because it combined the aspects I enjoyed of my graphic and typography and combined them together. Jewel Productions now has a smooth J and a beautiful art pallette to go with it. Also take note of the beautiful, distinct coloring.

Type Ad

This is the piece that I enjoyed taking on, but was very difficult as a whole. The project took an immense amount of patience as I had to individually finetune each letter to make it fit within the jewel. I picked words that would be representative of "shining like a diamond."

Horror Movie Poster

My last project of the year was this beautiful horror movie poster. I took some inspiration from the horror movie "Ouija Board" as the main girl in that movie always seemed creepy. The entire premise of this poster is that this girl comes killing people who don't reply back to texts.

Westview Student Planner Design

This was one of my favorite projects throughout the school year. We incorporated a starry theme to our design and allowed for an image of students looking up to the stars. It uses the image trace tool for some of the elements.

ADLIB

I'm a huge fan of the Big Bang Theory, so my ADLIB is primarily based off of the main characters from there. This was my first main project using photoshop. Many of the elements I used were basic, but some editing techniques were added.

Imaginary Product Template

This imaginary product template is based off our product, Watch Out. Watch Out is a watch that is geared towards spy companies which is why the packaging is very sleek and black and white. This design allows for the watch to be placed with a comfortable amount of space hugging it.

Imaginary Product 3D Box Design

The 3D Box Design really brings out how sleek the design looks. We tried to be discreet with our logotype and used different variations of it throughout the sides. In addition, we adjusted many of the graphics to make it seem more professional.

Personal Typeface

The "Helvetica" movie really inspired me to think more about the importance of typefaces in our society. We worked with three different types of typefaces: serif, san serif, and display. These three are the final typefaces I came up with and even incorporated in the t-shirt design.

Graphic Design T-shirt

This project was to make a t-shirt design, incorporating all of the Illustrator and Photoshop skills we knew. Our design tries to portray the creativity of a graphic design student and how they can progress to becoming this other worldy being. We could only use a few shades of colors for this project.

CD Packaging: Inner Cover

This was my last Graphic Design project and by far, my favorite. My partner and I went with the rap genre and were inspired by Cipe Pineles as seen by our front cover design. Our song titles are also in the background of the back cover, while our pictures are in front of it.

CD Packaging: Outer Cover

The outer cover includes both of our bios as well as an extra graphic of the diamond. Our CD is called Escape Tracks in order to represent rap songs that people could listen to in order to escape reality and shine on their own. This concluded my amazing term in Graphic Design!
